Restroom Finder

Restroom finder is a native mobile application written for iPhone and Android, designed to assist AC Transit operators and field employees to find the nearest restrooms around them, with comments and ratings by other employees.

Installing onto Android device

To install Restroom finder on your Android device, you will need to allow installation of apps from sources other than the Play Store. Open Android Settings then Security, pressing and accepting the Unknown sources feature.

Feedback

You can post a feedback for a restroom and rate the restroom based on your satisfaction. Follow these steps to write a feedback and rate a restroom:

1-      Make sure the restroom list is shown (tap on “Show List” if needed)

2-      Tap on icon for the restroom you want to write a feedback.

3-      Write your feedback, rate the restroom, then tap “OK”

You can also see other users’ feedback. Please note: the rating you see on the main screen is the average of the most recent feedback, which might be different from what you see on the screen.
